Biography

I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering Department, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Jiaya Jia in 2019. Before that, I received the B.E. degree in Information Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2015.

My research interest includes computer vision and deep learning.

Pinned: Highly optimized PyTorch codebases available for semantic segmentation semseg and panoptic segmentation UPSNet.

Fully self-attention based image recognition models SAN.
