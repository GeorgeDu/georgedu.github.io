Biography

I am currently a senior algorithm engineer in CloudMinds Technologies. I obtained my Master and PhD degrees of Computer Graphics from Beijing Normal Universtiy in Jun 2013 and Jan 2018, supervised by Prof. Zhongke Wu and Prof. Mingquan Zhou, respectively. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor degree of Electronic Engineering from Shandong Normal University in 2010.

My research interest includes 3D Vision, 6D object pose estimation and 3D point cloud registration. In my spare time, I write many technical articals and put them on my CSDN blogs as well as my WeChat Subscription “3D视觉前沿”.
