Biography

I am currently a researcher of State Key Laboratory of High-End Server and Storage Technology, Inspur Electronic Information Industry Co., Ltd. Before that, I worked in KE Holdings Inc. and CloudMinds Technologies. I obtained my Master and PhD degrees of Computer Graphics from Beijing Normal Universtiy in Jun 2013 and Jan 2018, supervised by Prof. Zhongke Wu and Prof. Mingquan Zhou, respectively.

My research interest includes 3D Vision related technologies, such as 3D shape generation, audio-driven 2D/3D face animation, 6D object pose estimation, point cloud registration and vision-based robotic grasping. In my spare time, I often write technical articals and put them on my CSDN blogs as well as my WeChat Subscription “3D视觉前沿”.
