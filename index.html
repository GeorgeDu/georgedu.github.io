Biography

I am currently a researcher in IEIT SYSTEMS Co., Ltd. Before that, I worked in KE Holdings Inc. and CloudMinds Technologies. I earned my Ph.D. in January 2018 from Beijing Normal Universtiy, supervised by Prof. Zhongke Wu and Prof. Mingquan Zhou.

Leveraging my background in 3D perception, multimodal video/3D generation, and robotics, I focus on developing data-driven Physical Foundation Models that enables AI systems to better understand and interact with the physical world.
